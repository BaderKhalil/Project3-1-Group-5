###
Question: Sarah rolls a six-sided die and flips a coin. The outcomes of rolling a 3 and getting heads are known as...

###
Answers:
(A) - Independent events 

(B) - Dependent events 

(C) - Complementary events 

(D) - Joint events 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Cannot identify events that are influenced by one another 

(B) - Assumes one event influences the outcome of the other 

(C) - Confuses complementary events with unrelated outcomes 

(D) - Misunderstands the nature of joint occurrences
