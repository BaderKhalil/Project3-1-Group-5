###
Question: In parallelogram \( W X Y Z \), which pairs of opposite sides are equal in length? ![Parallelogram with vertices labelled W, X, Y, and Z in a clockwise direction.]() 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( W X \) and \( Y Z \) 

(B) - \( W Z \) and \( X Y \) 

(C) - \( W Y \) and \( X Z \) 

(D) - All sides are equal 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Does not recognize that opposite sides of a parallelogram are equal 

(B) - Believes that the diagonally opposite sides of a parallelogram are equal rather than the parallel sides 

(C) - Confuses sides being opposite and those being adjacent in a parallelogram 

(D) - Incorrectly assumes a parallelogram is a rectangle with all sides equal
