###
Question: Which of the following represents " \( y \) is less than or equal to \( 15 \) "? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( y<15 \) 

(B) - \( y \geq 15 \) 

(C) - \( y \leq 15 \) 

(D) - \( y>15 \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Ignores the equal part of the inequality 

(B) - Confuses the direction of the inequality, thinking greater than means less than 

(C) - nan 

(D) - Misunderstands the inequality symbols, believing "greater than" includes all numbers
