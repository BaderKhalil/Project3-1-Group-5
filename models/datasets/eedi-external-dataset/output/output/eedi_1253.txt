###
Question: If a bar is divided into 8 equal parts and 3 parts are shaded blue, what fraction represents the shaded area? ![A bar split into 8 equal parts. Three parts are shaded blue.]()

###
Answers:
(A) - \( \frac{3}{8} \) 

(B) - \( \frac{5}{8} \) 

(C) - \( \frac{3}{5} \) 

(D) - \( \frac{1}{8} \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Correct answer, as it accurately reflects the shaded parts of the bar 

(B) - Misunderstands how many parts are shaded versus total parts 

(C) - Incorrectly applies fractions, does not recognize the total number of parts 

(D) - Fails to recognize that \( \frac{1}{8} \) represents only one part of the whole
