###
Question: What is \( 75\% \) expressed as a fraction? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( \frac{3}{4} \) 

(B) - \( \frac{15}{20} \) 

(C) - \( \frac{75}{25} \) 

(D) - \( \frac{7}{10} \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Correct answer, but may not be seen as obvious to some 

(B) - Incorrect simplification, thinking \( 15 \div 20 = 75 \) 

(C) - Incorrect use of a larger number divided, misunderstanding the conversion 

(D) - Divided incorrectly based on a misunderstanding of percentage values
