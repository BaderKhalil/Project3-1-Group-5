###
Question: Which inequality symbol makes the following statement true?
\(
\frac{3}{8} ? \frac{5}{12}
\)

###
Answers:
(A) - \( > \)

(B) - \( < \)

(C) - \( = \)

(D) - \( \approx \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Believes that larger numerators automatically mean larger fractions regardless of denominators 

(B) - Correctly identifies the smaller fraction but miscalculates based on the denominators 

(C) - Incorrectly thinks both fractions are equivalent without simplifying 

(D) - Assumes fractions are close in value without proper comparison
