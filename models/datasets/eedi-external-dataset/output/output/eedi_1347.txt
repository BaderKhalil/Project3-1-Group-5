Question: A survey of students in a classroom revealed their scores on a recent math test. What is the most appropriate title for the second column in the following table? 

\begin{tabular}{|c|c|l|}
\hline Score & Frequency & \\
\hline \( 70 \) & \( 5 \) & \\
\hline \( 75 \) & \( 8 \) & \\
\hline \( 80 \) & \( 3 \) & \\
\hline \( 85 \) & \( 4 \) & \\
\hline
\end{tabular} 

###
Answers:
(A) - Score + Frequency 

(B) - Score - Frequency 

(C) - Number of Students 

(D) - Score \( \div \) Frequency 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Adds instead of counts 

(B) - Subtracts rather than counts the occurrences 

(C) - Incorrectly identifies total instead of individual 

(D) - Divides rather than counts
