###
Question: Which of the following is the best measure of central tendency for the data set?
\[
3, 3, 4, 4, 5, 100
\] 

###
Answers:
(A) - Mean 

(B) - Median 

(C) - Mode 

(D) - All of the above 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Believes that the mean accurately represents the majority of the data despite outliers 

(B) - Incorrectly assumes that the median is always affected by extreme values 

(C) - Thinks the mode alone provides a full picture without considering the distribution of data 

(D) - Believes all measures of central tendency are equally valid regardless of the data distribution 

###
Subject: Averages (mean, median, mode) from a List of Data
###
ConstructName: Understand the limitations of the mean
###
CorrectAnswer: B
