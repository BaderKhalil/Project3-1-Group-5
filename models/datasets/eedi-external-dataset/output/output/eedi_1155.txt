###
Answers:
(A) - \( 1 \)

(B) - \( 2 \)

(C) - \( 4 \) 

(D) - \( 6 \)

###
Misconceptions:

(B) - Assumes the point marked by the arrow is higher than it actually is due to underestimating the spacing between numbers.

(C) - nan 

(D) - Incorrectly assumes that the distance between the marked numbers is equal without considering the gaps on the number line.
