###
Question: Factorise this expression, if possible:
\(
x^{2} + 7x
\)

###
Answers:
(A) - \( x(x + 7) \)

(B) - \( (x + 7)(x + 7) \)

(C) - \( (x + \sqrt{7})(x - \sqrt{7}) \)

(D) - Does not factorise

###

(A) - nan

(B) - When factorising a quadratic expression, mistakenly assumes both terms must be identical.

(C) - When factorising a quadratic without a non-variable term, tries to apply the double bracket method incorrectly.
