###
Question: What is the median of the following numbers?
\[
2, 4, 6, 8, 10
\] 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 4 \) 

(B) - \( 6 \) 

(C) - \( 8 \) 

(D) - \( 10 \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Finds the mean instead of the median 

(B) - Correctly identifies the middle value in an even numbered set, but incorrectly applies that to an odd numbered set 

(C) - Considers the last number as the median instead of identifying the middle value 

(D) - Assumes the highest value is the median without checking the distribution of values
