###
Question: A point, \( R \), has coordinates \( (3, 7) \). 
The point is reflected in the line \( y=7 \). 
What are the new coordinates of point \( R \) ?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( (3, 7) \)

(B) - \( (3, 14) \)

(C) - \( (0, 7) \)

(D) - \( (3, 0) \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - nan 

(B) - Thinks the reflection doubles the distance to the line

(C) - Thinks the reflection occurs in the x-axis instead of the y-axis

(D) - Thinks the y-coordinate is simply negated
