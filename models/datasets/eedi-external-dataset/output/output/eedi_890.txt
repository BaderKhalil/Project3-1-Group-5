###
Question: Multiply out the brackets: \( x(x + 5) \)

###
Answers:
(A) - \( x^{2} + 5 \)

(B) - \( x^{2} + 5x \)

(C) - \( 5x + x \)

(D) - \( x^{2} + 10 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Only combines the variable term with the constant, neglecting the multiplication of both terms.

(B) - Correct, as it appropriately applies the distributive property.

(C) - Incorrectly assumes the addition of \( x \) without properly applying the multiplication.

(D) - Incorrectly adds the constant term when multiplying instead of following the appropriate multiplication rule.
