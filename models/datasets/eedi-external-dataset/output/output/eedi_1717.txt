Question: Which of the following represents a valid first step to simplify the expression: 
\( 4x + 2(3 - x) = 10 \)

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 4x + 6 - 2x = 10 \)

(B) - \( 2(3 - x) = 10 - 4x \)

(C) - \( 4x + 6 - 2x = 4 \)

(D) - \( 4x + 6 - 3 = 10 \)

###

(A) - Incorrectly distributes the constant over the expression, miscalculating the operation.
