###
Question: The heights of five students in inches are recorded as follows:
\(
\begin{array}{llllll}
h & 56 & 62 & t & 68 & 74
\end{array}
\)
Write an expression for the range. 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( h - 74 \) 

(B) - \( t - 62 \) 

(C) - \( 74 - h \) 

(D) - \( \frac{300 + t + h}{5} \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - subtracts the highest from the lowest in the wrong order 

(B) - calculates the difference incorrectly as it does not involve both extremes 

(C) - nan 

(D) - calculates the average instead of the range
