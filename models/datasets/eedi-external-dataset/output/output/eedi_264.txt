Question: Which of the following represents " \( y \) is less than \( 4 \) "? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( y \geq 4 \) 

(B) - \( y < 4 \) 

(C) - \( y \leq 4 \) 

(D) - \( y > 4 \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Confuses greater than or equal to with less than, not understanding the direction of the inequality 

(B) - nan 

(C) - Mixes up the "less than" symbol with "less than or equal to," incorrectly including 4 

(D) - Thinks "greater than" means includes a value less than, misunderstanding the comparative nature of the symbols
