###
Question: Mark wants to estimate the total cost of painting his bedroom, which measures \( 3.7 \mathrm{~m} \) by \( 4.5 \mathrm{~m} \). What calculation will best estimate the area to be painted?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 4 \times 4 \)

(B) - \( 4 \times 5 \)

(C) - \( 3 \times 5 \)

(D) - \( 3 \times 4 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Uses incorrect rounding that assumes both dimensions are too small.

(B) - Rounds one measurement up, assuming an inaccurate excess in area.

(C) - Rounds one length down too far, underestimating the painted area.

(D) - Rounds down both dimensions, leading to a significant underestimation.

###
Subject: Estimation
###
ConstructName: Round decimals to the nearest whole number
###
CorrectAnswer: B
