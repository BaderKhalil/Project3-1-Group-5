###
Question: What is the solution set for the inequality?

\(
2x-5 > 3
\)

###
Answers:
(A) - \( \{x > 4\} \) 

(B) - \( \{x < 4\} \) 

(C) - \( \{x > 1\} \) 

(D) - \( \{x \leq 1\} \) 

###
