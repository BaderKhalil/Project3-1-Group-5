###
Question: Which number is closest to the median of the following numbers?
\(
3.1, \quad \frac{5}{2}, \quad 4.0, \quad \frac{7}{3}
\)

###
Answers:
(A) - \( \frac{5}{2} \)

(B) - 3.1

(C) - \( \frac{7}{3} \)

(D) - 4.0

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Misunderstands how to organize data to find the median, focusing on an average rather than the middle value. 

(B) - Incorrectly assumes the median is always one of the original numbers without calculating it.

(C) - Confuses the positions of numbers in the ordered list, potentially thinking the largest value is the median.

(D) - Fails to identify that the median is the middle value and interprets that it must be the largest number.
