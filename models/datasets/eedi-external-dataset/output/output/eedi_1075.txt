###
Answers:
(A) - A 

(B) - B

(D) - D 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Mixes up less than and greater than symbols

(B) - Confuses the location of the boundary line

(C) - Assumes all points in the quadrant satisfy the inequalities

(D) - Incorrectly interprets \( \leq \) and \( \geq \) as \( < \) and \( > \)
