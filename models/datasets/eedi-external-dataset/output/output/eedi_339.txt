###
Question: In the expression \( 4x + 5z - 2 \), which of the following best describes the component \( 5z \)?

###
Answers:
(A) - Coefficient

(B) - Constant

(C) - Variable

(D) - Term

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Confuses a term with a coefficient 

(B) - Confuses a component with a constant 

(C) - Confuses a term with a variable 

(D) - nan 

###
Subject: Writing Expressions
###
ConstructName: Recognise and name a term
###
CorrectAnswer: D
