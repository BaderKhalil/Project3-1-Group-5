Question: Jamie is at a bookstore.
Each book costs \( £ 8.50 \).
Jamie has \( £ 50 \) to spend.
What is the maximum number of books he can buy?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 3 \)

(B) - \( 5 \)

(C) - \( 6 \)

(D) - \( 4 \)

(D) - Assumes he can buy exactly 4 without calculating the total cost properly.
