###
Question: What is \( 45 \) out of \( 60 \) as a percentage?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 60 \% \)

(B) - \( 75 \% \)

(C) - \( 80 \% \)

(D) - \( 90 \% \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Thinks the percentage is calculated by taking the first number as a whole and not converting it through division. 

(B) - Miscalculates using an incorrect reference value, thinking \( 45 \) is three-quarters of \( 60 \).

(C) - Assumes the percentage can be found by rounding up instead of accurately calculating the decimal equivalent.

(D) - Believes that the fraction it represents is equivalent to a higher percentage than it should be.
