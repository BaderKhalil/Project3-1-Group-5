Question: What is the value of C? ![A vertical number-line with 6 horizontal, equally spaced, dashes representing the position of numbers. "-3" next to the bottom dash and "5" next to the top dash are the only numbers marked on the line. An arrow, labelled "C" is pointing at the second dash from the bottom.]() 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 0 \) 

(B) - \( 2 \) 

(C) - \( 1 \) 

(D) - \( -2 \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Assumes the value is zero without considering the actual dashes marked on the line. 

(B) - Counts incorrectly from the bottom number to identify the dashed position. 

(C) - nan 

(D) - Incorrectly assumes a negative value when the position is above the bottom number.
