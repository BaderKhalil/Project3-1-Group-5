###
Question: What is the median of the following numbers?
\[
10, 3, 7, 4, 6, 8
\]

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 6.5 \)

(B) - \( 4 \)

(C) - \( 7 \)

(D) - \( 8 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Correctly calculated the median as the average of the two middle numbers 

(B) - Selects the smallest value instead of finding the median 

(C) - Assumes the middle value is simply the largest of the smaller half of the dataset 

(D) - Confuses the median with the maximum value in the dataset
