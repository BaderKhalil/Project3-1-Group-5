Question: The chart shows the rain levels in Sarah's backyard over a \( 24 \) hour period.

During which \( 4 \) hour period did the rain level increase the most? ![A graph showing the rain levels in Sarah's backyard over a 24 hours period. At 00:00 the rain level is 0 mm, at 04:00 it is 5 mm, at 08:00 it is 10 mm, at 12:00 it is 20 mm, at 16:00 it is 25 mm, at 20:00 it is 30 mm and at 00:00 it is 35 mm.]()
