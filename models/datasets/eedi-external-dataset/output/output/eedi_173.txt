###
Question: A gardener mixes two types of fertilizer in the ratio \( 4: 1 \). What is this ratio in the form \( 1: n \)?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 1: 0.25 \)

(B) - \( 1: 4 \)

(C) - \( 1: 5 \)

(D) - \( 1: 3 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Incorrectly assumes that the smaller amount is always the second part of the ratio. 

(B) - Correctly understands the conversion of the ratio from \( 4:1 \) to \( 1:4 \).

(C) - Incorrectly adds the parts of the ratio instead of simplifying.

(D) - Incorrectly divides the first part of the ratio without adjusting the second part proportionally.
