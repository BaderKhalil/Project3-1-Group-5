Question: A triangle has the following angles: \( 60\degree, 30\degree, 90\degree \)  
Mark claims the triangle is equilateral  
Lucy claims the triangle is right-angled  
Who is correct?

###
Answers:
(A) - Only Mark

(B) - Both Mark and Lucy

(C) - Only Lucy

(D) - Neither are correct 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Does not know that in an equilateral triangle, all angles must be equal

(B) - Misunderstands the definitions of equilateral and right-angled triangles

(C) - Correctly identifies Lucy's claim but may not recognize the property of equilateral triangles

(D) - Does not recognize that a right angle is indeed part of the triangle's angles
