###
Question: A parallelogram must have... 

###
Answers:
(A) - Opposite sides equal

(B) - All angles right angles

(C) - All sides equal

(D) - Opposite angles equal 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Does not realize that opposite sides being equal is a defining property of parallelograms.

(B) - Confuses parallelograms with rectangles, which have right angles.

(C) - Confuses parallelograms with rhombuses, which have all sides equal.

(D) - Does not recognize that while opposite angles are equal, it's not the defining feature of a parallelogram.
