###
Question: Which of the following expressions would correctly calculate the area of this rectangle? ![A rectangle with length 10m and width 4m]() 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 10 + 4 \)

(B) - \( 10 \times 4 \)

(C) - \( 10 - 4 \)

(D) - \( 10 \times 4 \times 2 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Adds the dimensions instead of multiplying them to find the area

(B) - nan

(C) - Subtracts the dimensions, which does not apply to area calculation

(D) - Multiplies by 2, incorrectly treating it as a volume calculation instead of area
