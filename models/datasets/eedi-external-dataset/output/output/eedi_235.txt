###
Question: A bag contains \( 15 \) marbles.

\( 5 \) of the marbles are red

\( 7 \) of the marbles are yellow

The rest of the marbles are green

What is the probability of choosing a green marble? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 3 \)

(B) - \( \frac{1}{5} \)

(C) - \( \frac{7}{15} \)

(D) - \( \frac{3}{15} \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Forgets the denominator in probability 

(B) - Thinks the probability must represent a smaller count than needed 

(C) - Confuses counts of non-representative colors with the total count 

(D) - Confuses the fraction representation by using an incorrect count of green marbles
