###
Question: The median of these five numbers is \( 10 \).
\(
\begin{array}{ccccc}
8 & 14 & 12 & ? & 6
\end{array}
\)
Find the value of the missing number.

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 9 \)

(B) - \( 10 \)

(C) - \( 11 \)

(D) - \( 5 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Assumes the median already includes the missing number as part of the set 

(B) - Misunderstands the definition of median, thinking it's the average 

(C) - Incorrectly assumes that the missing number must be one of the existing numbers to achieve the median 

(D) - Fails to consider that placing \( 5 \) in the set would not yield \( 10 \) as a median
