Question: A number is divided by three, then increased by two, resulting in \( 9 \). Which of the following equations describes this situation? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( \frac{n}{3} + 2 = 9 \) 

(B) - \( n + 3 = 9 \) 

(C) - \( \frac{n+2}{3} = 9 \) 

(D) - \( n + 2 \div 3 = 9 \) 

###
