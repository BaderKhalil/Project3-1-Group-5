###
Question: What is the range of the following set of data?
\[
10 \mathrm{~m}, \quad 15 \mathrm{~m}, \quad 12 \mathrm{~m}, \quad 5 \mathrm{~m}
\] 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 10 \mathrm{~m} \) 

(B) - \( 15 \mathrm{~m} \) 

(C) - \( 5 \mathrm{~m} \) 

(D) - \( 20 \mathrm{~m} \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Thinks that the range is simply the standard deviation or average of the values 

(B) - Incorrectly identifies the largest number in the set as the range 

(C) - Believes the range is just the smallest number in the set 

(D) - Thinks that calculating the range requires adding the largest and smallest values
