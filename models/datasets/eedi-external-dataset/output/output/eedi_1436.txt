###
Question: What is the equation of the \( y \) axis?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( y=1 \) 

(B) - \( y=0 \) 

(C) - \( x=0 \) 

(D) - \( y=x \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Thinks y = 1 is a representation of the y axis 

(B) - Confuses the equations of horizontal and vertical lines 

(C) - Correctly identifies the y axis but confuses its equation with that of the x axis 

(D) - Thinks y = x represents an axis instead of a line through the origin
