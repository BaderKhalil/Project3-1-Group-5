###
Question: A farmer has \( 50 \) apples. He gives away \( 18 \) apples to his neighbors. How many apples does he have left? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 32 \) 

(B) - \( 68 \) 

(C) - \( 18 \) 

(D) - \( 50 \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Correctly subtracts the apples given away but miscalculates the result. 
(B) - Adds the apples given away instead of subtracting them from the total.
(C) - Misunderstands the problem as asking for the number of apples given away instead of remaining.
(D) - Fails to account for the apples given away and believes the total remains unchanged.
