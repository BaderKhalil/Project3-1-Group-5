###
Question: If a pizza is cut into \( 10 \) equal slices, how many slices need to be eaten to consume \( 70\% \) of the pizza?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 5 \)

(B) - \( 7 \)

(C) - \( 3 \)

(D) - \( 10 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Misunderstands that eating 5 slices is sufficient without calculating the percentage.

(B) - Correctly calculates 70% of the total slices, which is \( 7 \) slices.

(C) - Confuses the percentage with a smaller fraction, not realizing \( 3 \) slices only accounts for \( 30\% \).

(D) - Incorrectly assumes that eating all the slices would be required for a percentage that is less than \( 100\% \).
