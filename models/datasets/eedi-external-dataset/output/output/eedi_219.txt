###
Question: The following chart displays the number of books read by a group of children over the summer.

Which number represents the mode of books read? \begin{tabular}{|c|c|}
\hline Number of books & Frequency \\
\hline \( 0 \) & \( 5 \) \\
\hline \( 1 \) & \( 7 \\
\hline \( 2 \) & \( 4 \) \\
\hline \( 3 \) & \( 6 \) \\
\hline \( 4 \) & \( 8 \) \\
\hline
\end{tabular} 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 4 \)

(B) - \( 2 \)

(C) - \( 1 \)

(D) - \( 3 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Correct choice but may not understand how the mode differs from overall averages.

(B) - Finds the median instead of the mode.

(C) - Believes the mode is simply the most frequently occurring number, not realizing the counts matter.

(D) - Misunderstands that the mode must be the highest frequency and incorrectly includes other values.
