Question: Sarah and John are debating types of angles.  
Sarah claims an angle of \( 90^\circ \) is an acute angle.  
John asserts that an angle of \( 45^\circ \) is an obtuse angle.  
Who is correct?  

###
Answers:  
(A) - Only Sarah  

(B) - Only John  

(C) - Both Sarah and John  

(D) - Neither is correct  

###
Misconceptions:  
(A) - Mistakes classification of angles; acute angle is defined correctly.  

(B) - Misunderstands angle classifications; \( 45^\circ \) is actually acute, not obtuse.  

(C) - Fails to recognize the correct definitions of acute and obtuse angles.  

(D) - Incorrectly believes both statements are wrong.
