Question: A pie chart represents the percentage of different fruit sales at a local market. If the section representing Apples takes up 30% of the pie, what percentage of the pie is allocated to Bananas if the section for Bananas is twice the size of the section for Apples?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 20\% \)

(B) - \( 60\% \)

(C) - \( 30\% \)

(D) - \( 50\% \)
