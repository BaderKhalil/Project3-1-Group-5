Question: In the figure below, \( A \) and \( B \) are points where the transversal \( L \) intersects the two parallel lines, \( K \) and \( M \). What angle is corresponding to angle ABL? ![A pair of parallel lines extending horizontally. K is the top line, and M is the bottom line. A red transversal line L intersects lines K and M. The intersection at K is point A and at M is point B. The angle ABL is marked in red.]() 

###
Answers:
(A) - BLM 

(B) - ALB 

(C) - AML 

(D) - KAB 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Confuses corresponding and vertically opposite angles 

(B) - Confuses supplementary angles with corresponding angles 

(C) - Confuses corresponding and alternate angles 

(D) - Confuses corresponding angles with angles formed by different lines
