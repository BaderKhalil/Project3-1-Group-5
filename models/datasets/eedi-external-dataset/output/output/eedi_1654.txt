###
Question: If \( y = \frac{5}{x} \), what is the value of \( y \) when \( x = 5 \)? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 1 \)

(B) - \( 0 \)

(C) - \( 5 \)

(D) - \( \frac{1}{5} \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Believes that dividing 5 by itself results in 1, understanding it correctly. 

(B) - Thinks that any division involving a number results in zero. 

(C) - Incorrectly believes that the output remains the same as the input value. 

(D) - Presumes that the value of y will simply reflect 5 scaled down by a factor related to the fraction format.
