\hline Chocolate & \( 12 \) \\
\hline Vanilla & \( 8 \) \\
\hline Red Velvet & \( 10 \) \\
\hline
\end{tabular}

\begin{tabular}{|c|c|}

\hline Chocolate & \( 36 \) \\
\hline Vanilla & \( 48 \) \\
\hline Red Velvet & \( 36 \) \\
\hline
\end{tabular} Which bakery's results are more reliable?
