Question: A local park has two different sections: a rectangular flower bed and a trapezoidal picnic area. The flower bed is 5 meters wide and 10 meters long, while the picnic area has bases of 8 meters and 5 meters, with a height of 4 meters. Which calculation correctly finds the total area of both sections? 

###
Answers:
(A) - Lucy: \( 5 \times 10 + \frac{1}{2} \times (8 + 5) \times 4 \) 

(B) - Mark: \( 10 \times 5 + 8 \times 4 \) 

(C) - Emma: \( 5 \times 4 + 5 \times 8 \) 

(D) - They are all correct
