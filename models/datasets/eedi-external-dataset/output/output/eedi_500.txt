###
Question: What is the next term in the sequence?

\[
15, 10, 5, \ldots
\] 

###
Answers:
(A) - The next term is \( 0 \)

(B) - The next term is \( 10 \)

(C) - The next term is \( 5 \)

(D) - The next term is \( -5 \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Confuses the repeated application of decreasing by 5 with always subtracting an extra 5. 

(B) - Misinterprets the pattern as not changing when it actually is decreasing.

(C) - Incorrectly assumes that the previous term remains the next term.

(D) - Sees the linear pattern but goes too far in the direction of subtraction.
