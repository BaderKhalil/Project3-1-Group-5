###
Question: If I am facing North, how many degrees clockwise must I turn to face West?

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 90^{\circ} \)

(B) - \( 180^{\circ} \)

(C) - \( 270^{\circ} \)

(D) - \( 360^{\circ} \)

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Confuses the direction with the smallest angle needed and mixes up reasoning about clockwise and anticlockwise. 

(B) - Underestimates the necessary turn to reach from North to West.

(C) - nan 

(D) - Does not account for returning to the original position instead of stopping at West.
