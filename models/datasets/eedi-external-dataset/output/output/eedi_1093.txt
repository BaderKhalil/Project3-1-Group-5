Question: This table shows the number of books read by a group of students in a month.

What is the modal number of books read? \begin{tabular}{|c|c|l|}
\hline Books Read & Frequency & \\
\hline \( 0 \) & \( 2 \) & \\
\hline \( 1 \) & \( 5 \) & \\
\hline \( 2 \) & \( 3 \) & \\
\hline \( 3 \) & \( 1 \) & \\
\hline
\end{tabular} 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 1 \) 

(B) - \( 2.5 \) 

(C) - \( 5 \) 

(D) - \( 3 \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - nan 

(B) - Calculates the mean instead of the mode 

(C) - Thinks the mode refers to the highest frequency value rather than the number of books 

(D) - Considers all values rather than looking at which value has the highest frequency
