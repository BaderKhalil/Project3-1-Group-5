\[
55, \quad 78, \quad 62, \quad 85, \quad 91, \quad 74
\]

Sarah claims that if the lowest score increases by \( 10 \), the range will decrease. Mike argues that if the highest score decreases by \( 10 \), the range will also decrease. Who do you think is correct?

###
Answers:
(A) - Only Sarah 

(B) - Only Mike 

(C) - Both Sarah and Mike 

(D) - Neither is correct

(D) - Thinks that both changes will have no impact on the range
