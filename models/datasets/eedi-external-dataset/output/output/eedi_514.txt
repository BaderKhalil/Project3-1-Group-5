Question: What is the formula used to calculate the area of a rectangle if the length is 50 cm and the width is 20 cm? 

###
Answers:
(A) - \( 50 + 20 \) 

(B) - \( 50 \times 20 \) 

(C) - \( \frac{50 \times 20}{2} \) 

(D) - \( 50 - 20 \) 

###
Misconceptions:
(A) - Has added the length and width instead of multiplying to find area 

(B) - nan 

(C) - Has divided the product by 2, which is incorrect as area of a rectangle does not require division 

(D) - Has subtracted width from the length instead of multiplying them to find area
